Summary

The Senior Staff Data Engineer is responsible for leading data infrastructure initiatives and driving operational excellence across the data engineering function. This role ensures reliable data ingestion, orchestration, and application delivery while supporting organizational analytics goals and fostering a culture of technical excellence and collaboration. The Senior Staff Data Engineer serves as a strategic partner to analytics, engineering, and business intelligence teams.

Responsibilities

Designing, implementing, and maintaining scalable data pipelines and orchestration workflows using Airflow and modern data stack technologies
Building and optimizing data ingestion processes from diverse sources to support analytics and business applications
Developing and maintaining analytics engineering workflows using dbt, SQL, and Python to transform raw data into analytics-ready models
Leading code reviews, establishing best practices, and mentoring junior engineers on data DevOps principles and tooling
Collaborating with analytics and BI teams to understand requirements and deliver robust data solutions
Monitoring pipeline performance, data quality metrics, and system health while implementing proactive improvements
Managing data infrastructure as code, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment automation for data applications
Documenting technical specifications, architectural decisions, to ensure knowledge sharing and maintainability

Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, Information Systems, or equivalent experience
7+ years of experience in data engineering, analytics engineering, or DevOps roles with demonstrated progression in technical complexity
Advanced proficiency in Python for data processing, automation, and application development
Expert-level SQL skills and hands-on experience with dbt for analytics engineering
Experience with workflow orchestration tools, preferably Apache Airflow, for managing complex data pipelines
Strong understanding of data warehousing concepts, ETL/ELT patterns, and modern data stack technologies
Proven ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams and translate business requirements into technical solutions
Excellent problem-solving skills with a track record of troubleshooting complex data issues and implementing scalable solutions
Commitment to continuous learning, staying current with emerging data technologies, and sharing knowledge with the team

About Us

Chobani is a food maker with a mission of making high-quality and nutritious food accessible to more people, while elevating our communities and making the world a healthier place. In short: making good food for all. In support of this mission, Chobani is a purpose-driven, people-first, food-and-wellness-focused company, and has been since its founding in 2005 by Hamdi Ulukaya, an immigrant to the U.S. The Company manufactures yogurt, oat milk, and creamers – Chobani yogurt is America's No.1 yogurt brand, made with natural ingredients without artificial preservatives. Following the 2023 acquisition of La Colombe, a leading coffee roaster with a shared commitment to quality, craftmanship and impact, the Company began selling cold-pressed espresso and lattes on tap at cafés nationwide, as well as Ready to Drink (RTD) coffee beverages at retail. In 2025, Chobani acquired Daily Harvest, a modern brand offering consumers nutritious, delicious and convenient ready-to-make meals.

Chobani uses food as a force for good in the world – putting humanity first in everything it does. The company's philanthropic efforts prioritize giving back to its communities and beyond. Chobani manufactures its products in New York, Idaho, Michigan and Australia, and its products are available throughout North America and distributed in Australia and other select markets.
