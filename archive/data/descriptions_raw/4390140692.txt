Senior Data Engineer

100% Remote

Overview

As a Senior Data Engineer, you will work on data systems and services alongside other data engineers to deliver projects and platforms critical to the Platform Ventures business. This role requires strong technical leadership, hands‑on expertise, and the ability to solve complex data challenges while helping the organization continue to build scalable, high‑quality solutions for customers.

Role Summary

The Senior Data Engineer will design and develop data pipelines, data marts, and schemas to support enterprise data warehousing environments and advanced analytical requirements.

Data Platform & Architecture

Design and implement scalable data lakehouse architectures using AWS S3, Delta Lake, and Redshift
Build and maintain high‑performance data pipelines to ingest, transform, and curate large datasets
Define best practices for data modeling, partitioning, and storage optimization within a lakehouse and medallion architecture
Develop and maintain data cataloging and metadata management solutions

Data Engineering & Processing

Develop ETL and ELT pipelines using Python, PySpark, and AWS Glue
Build and maintain robust batch and streaming data pipelines
Implement transformation workflows using DBT
Optimize performance for Apache Spark workloads and Amazon Redshift queries

Workflow Orchestration

Design and maintain workflow orchestration using Apache Airflow
Monitor pipeline performance and implement alerting, failure handling, and recovery mechanisms

Data Governance & Quality

Implement data validation, lineage, and monitoring frameworks
Maintain data documentation and integrate with enterprise data catalogs
Ensure data reliability, consistency, and security across all platforms

DevOps & Automation

Implement CI/CD pipelines for data workflows
Automate testing, deployment, and monitoring of data pipelines
Use infrastructure‑as‑code practices to maintain reproducible and scalable environments

Collaboration

Work closely with data consumers, analysts, and platform engineers
Enable self‑service analytics through well‑structured datasets and semantic data models

Minimum Qualifications

7+ years of experience in data warehousing or OLAP environments
7+ years of experience in multidimensional database architecture, data modeling, and design
5+ years of advanced SQL experience
3+ years of Python development experience
2+ years of experience with Amazon Redshift
2+ years of experience with NoSQL databases
Experience managing teams and vendor partners
Strong understanding of web development principles and techniques
Experience with AWS Glue, Lambda, Step Functions, and Apache Airflow
Experience ingesting data from APIs
Strong background in dimensional data modeling
Experience with formal systems development life cycle methodologies
Experience implementing CI/CD pipelines for data workflows
Experience working with data catalogs and metadata management solutions

Desired Qualifications

Experience operating and supporting AWS data warehouse platforms
Experience with Tableau, Amazon QuickSight, Looker, big data platforms, and real‑time BI solutions
Experience with streaming data technologies
